Claims sourced here: forward-deployed engineers embed inside a customer's environment and write production code in the customer's own systems, owning implementation rather than writing advisory reports; the model originated ~20 years ago at Palantir and is now being copied by the AI labs; the enterprise-AI bottleneck is data integration, workflow redesign and organizational change inside messy legacy estates, "not the model"; FDE job postings rose ~800% between January and September 2025. Quote — "Forward deployed engineers are the invoice for making AI real." — Sanchit Vir Gogia, Chief Analyst, Greyhound Research (via CIO).

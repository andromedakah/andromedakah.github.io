- Axis Intelligence, "EU AI Act News 2026: The Revised Timeline, August Enforcement & Omnibus Deal Explained" — https://axis-intelligence.com/eu-ai-act-news/

Claims sourced here: Gartner agent-embedding and project-cancellation forecasts (as reported); MIT NANDA 95% figure (as reported); EU AI Act GPAI penalty powers and Article 50 transparency obligations in force from 2 August 2026; maximum GPAI penalty €15M or 3% of global annual turnover; under the Digital Omnibus (provisional agreement reached 7 May 2026, pending formal adoption) the high-risk Annex III deadline is deferred to 2 December 2027, while 2 August 2026 remains a live enforcement date for GPAI penalties and Article 50.
